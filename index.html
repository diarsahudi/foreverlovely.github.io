About Us

Forever Lovely is a pop duo/band fronted by twin sisters Forever Julia (guitar, piano) and Lovely Luna (piano, guitar). Originally from Yogyakarta, Indonesia, where they were born on October 17, 2005, the duo is now based in the Yogyakarta area. They first gained attention with their single "Usah" on YouTube.

The duo's music is produced by their father, Diar Sahudi, who is also a singer and songwriter. All of their music is recorded in the family's own music studio. For live concerts, the band typically features a 5-person lineup, with Julia mainly on guitar and Luna on piano, supported by additional musicians on other instruments.
